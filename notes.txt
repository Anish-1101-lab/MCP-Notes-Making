1. Octopuses have three hearts, blue blood, and nine brains - one central brain and eight mini-brains, one for each arm.
2. Naked mole rats can live up to 30+ years, are immune to cancer, and feel no pain from acid or heat.
3. Mantis shrimp can punch with the force of a bullet and see colors humans can't even imagine.
4. Axolotls can regenerate entire limbs, parts of their brain, and even portions of their heart.
5. Tardigrades (water bears) can survive in space, withstand temperatures from near absolute zero to 300°F, and live without water for decades.
6. Elephants can recognize themselves in mirrors, mourn their dead, and use tools - signs of high intelligence and self-awareness.
7. Hummingbirds can fly backward, upside down, and hover in place. Their hearts can beat up to 1,260 times per minute.
8. Pistol shrimp create a bubble that reaches 8,500°F when they snap their claw - almost as hot as the surface of the sun.
9. Ravens can solve complex puzzles, mimic human speech, and hold grudges against specific people for years.
10. Jellyfish have existed for over 650 million years - before dinosaurs and even trees - and some species are biologically immortal.
